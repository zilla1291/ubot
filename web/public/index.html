Getting Started Guide

Step 1: Get a Voucher

Contact us via Telegram @unfilteredg to purchase a voucher key. Once you have a voucher, you can deploy unlimited bots during its validity period.

Step 2: Connect WhatsApp

Use either the QR code or pairing code method to link your WhatsApp account:

  • QR Code: Open WhatsApp → Settings → Linked Devices → Link a Device → Scan QR
  • Pairing Code: Enter your phone number and enter the provided code in WhatsApp

Step 3: Configure Features

Choose which bot features you want to enable. You can toggle them on/off anytime from the management dashboard.

Step 4: Deploy

Click "Deploy Bot" and your bot will be deployed to our VPS. Deployment usually takes 1-2 minutes.

FAQ

Can I use one voucher multiple times?

No, each voucher is single-use for security purposes. Once deployed, the voucher is marked as used and cannot be reused.

How long does deployment take?

Typically 1-2 minutes. You can monitor the status in real-time on the dashboard.

Can I modify features after deployment?

Yes! You can toggle features on/off anytime from the Manage section.

What happens if deployment fails?

Contact support @unfilteredg. Your voucher will not be consumed in case of deployment failure.
